RESPIRATORY HEALTH

Because our breathing is ongoing, we sometimes forget how it can impact our daily lives, for the better or for the worse.

Find out how your pharmacist can help if you have respiratory health issues, whether mild or chronic.

Services from your Pharmacist

Several services* related to respiratory health are offered in pharmacy:

  • Spirometry (assessment of lung function).
  • Vaccination against flu, COVID-19, Respiratory syncytial virus (RSV) and pneumococcal infection.
  • Advice for smoking cessation.

*These services are offered at participating pharmacies only.
