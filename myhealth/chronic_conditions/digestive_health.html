DIGESTIVE HEALTH

When our body is working in order, we rarely give the digestive system a second thought. Still, it often needs a bit of care to keep doing what it does best: fuel our body, day in and day out. Find out how your pharmacy can help!

Services from your Pharmacist

Several services* related to digestive health are offered in pharmacy:

  • Advice on over-the-counter medications to help with digestive issues.
  • Prescription of medications for gastric reflux, nausea and vomiting.
  • Prescription of medications for diarrhea, constipation and hemorrhoids.
  • Prescription of medications that protect the stomach when treatments cause irritation.

*These services are offered at participating stores only.
