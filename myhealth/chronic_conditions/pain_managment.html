My Health: Pain Management

PAIN MANAGEMENT

While pain is a very subjective experience and differs from one person to another, everyone agrees that pain is not a welcomed guest in their daily life. Effectively managing pain becomes a crucial aspect of maintaining a healthy lifestyle, and your pharmacist can be a supportive companion on this journey.

Find your pharmacy

Services from your Pharmacist

Several services* related to pain management are offered in pharmacy:

  • Advice on over-the-counter medications to help with pain management.
  • Therapy adjustment to relieve acute and chronic pain.
  • Prescription of medications to relieve pain caused by periods.

*These services are offered at participating stores only.

TREATING LOWER BACK PAIN

Many things can cause low back pain. Sometimes, the exact cause of the pain is not known. When left untreated, it can become chronic and can have a significant impact on quality of life. That is why it is important to do something about it.
