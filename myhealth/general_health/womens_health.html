SERVICES FROM YOUR PHARMACIST

Several services* related to women's health are offered in pharmacy:

  • Evaluation and prescription for vaginal and urinary tract infections.
  • Evaluation and prescription for yeast infections.
  • Advice and follow-up on gestational diabetes.
  • Relief for pregnancy-related symptoms.
  • Advice on vitamins (for pregnancy and menopause).

*These services are offered at participating stores only.
