SUPPORT FROM YOUR PHARMACIST

Your pharmacist can help you and give you advice related to various health conditions common in men:

  • Erectile dysfunction
  • Cardiovascular health
  • Prostate diseases
  • Mental Health

Most pharmacists offer private consultations on site if you want to discuss private and confidential matters - just ask them!

TESTICULAR CANCER

Among all types of cancer, testicular cancer is the most common cancer among young Canadian men between the ages of 15 and 29. Fortunately, it is also the most treatable. Discover the importance of early detection.
