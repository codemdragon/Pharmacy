DIABETES

Diabetes is a chronic disease that can't be cured, but it can be treated and managed. Learn more about diabetes and discover how your pharmacist can help.

DIGESTIVE HEALTH

When our body is working in order, we rarely give the digestive system a second thought. Still, it often needs a bit of care to keep doing what it does best: fuel our body, day in and day out. Find out how your pharmacy can help!

Read more

HEART HEALTH

According to Statistics Canada, heart disease is the second-leading cause of mortality in the country, accounting for nearly one in five deaths. It is therefore important to take care of your heart to live a long and healthy life. Your pharmacist can help you prevent complications and manage your cardiovascular health.

Read more

PAIN MANAGEMENT

While pain is a very subjective experience and differs from one person to another, everyone agrees that pain is not a welcomed guest in their daily life. Effectively managing pain becomes a crucial aspect of maintaining a healthy lifestyle, and your pharmacist can be a supportive companion on this journey.

Read more

RESPIRATORY HEALTH

Because our breathing is ongoing, we sometimes forget how it can impact our daily lives, for the better or for the worse.
