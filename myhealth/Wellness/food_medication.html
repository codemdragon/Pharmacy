FOOD & MEDICATION

Food & Medication

With medication, there is cause to pose and rethink the way we eat and when. Food can interact with your medication in many ways, and when combined, can be source of some undesirable effects.

Your pharmacist can help you figure it all out, to get the most of your medication therapy with the best eating.

Find your pharmacy

PHARMACIST'S TIPS

Never underestimate the effect of food on your treatment. When your pharmacist gives you specific instructions regarding food, be sure to follow them! Otherwise, your treatment may not be fully effective or may even lead to side effects that could have been avoided.

Always check with your pharmacist!
