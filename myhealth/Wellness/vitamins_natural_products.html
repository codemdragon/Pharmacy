Vitamins, Minerals & Natural Products

No matter your age, your body needs vitamins and minerals to ensure all your vital functions. You will most likely meet your daily requirements by eating a healthy diet, but sometimes, a little boost doesn’t hurt - and your pharmacist can guide you in choosing the right product for you.

PHARMACIST TIPS

Talk to your pharmacist if you consider taking vitamins and natural products. He or she will give you advice related to many health conditions:

  • Pregnancy and menopause
  • Prevention of osteoporosis
  • Improvement of digestive health
  • Improvement of sleep quality
  • Moderation of potential interactions between medications, vitamins and natural products.
