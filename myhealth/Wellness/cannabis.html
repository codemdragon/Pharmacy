PHARMACIST TIPS

Your pharmacist is an essential member of your healthcare team. They can provide you with insight and relevant information to help you make decisions. They are there to guide you through your therapy choices without judgment or bias.

Your pharmacist has been managing your medication therapy for a long time. They naturally know it well and can advise on avoiding potential medication interactions and minimizing adverse effects.

Don’t hesitate to reach out to your local pharmacist on cannabis-related questions or concerns.
