COMFORT & SAFETY

Taking good care of your health is important at all ages, but perhaps even more so in later years. And if you are a caregiver, it's important that you fulfill your role without putting your own health at risk.

It's reassuring to know that comfort and safety are within reach and that your pharmacist can help.

FOOD & MEDICATION

With medication, there is cause to pose and rethink the way we eat and when. Food can interact with your medication in many ways, and when combined, can be source of some undesirable effects.

Your pharmacist can help you figure it all out, to get the most of your medication therapy with the best eating.

MENTAL HEALTH

1 in 3 Canadians will experience a mental health problem in their lifetime. Mental health disorders are challenging for people living with them as well as their loved ones, but these conditions can be overcome with appropriate treatment and help of your pharmacist.

SKIN HEALTH
